# Working at Incubyte

## Key Notes

* **Software Craftsmanship:** Build software that is clean, maintainable, testable, and easy to evolve. The goal is to create long-term value, not just complete a task.
* **Engineering Practices:** Quality is everyone's responsibility. Practices like TDD, Pair Programming, Continuous Integration, and DevOps help improve both the software and the development process.
* **Consultant Mindset:** Understand the customer's business and the problem behind the requirement. Suggest better solutions when possible instead of simply implementing requested features.
* **Ownership:** Take responsibility for the complete outcome of your work, communicate proactively, and honour commitments.
* **Continuous Learning:** Learning is part of everyday work through reading, mentoring, knowledge sharing, feedback, and regular practice.
* **Collaboration:** Work closely with teammates, share knowledge, and help each other grow to deliver better results.
