# Lean Thinking

## Key Notes

* Lean Thinking focuses on delivering **maximum value to the customer** while minimizing activities that do not add value.
* Waste is anything that does not contribute to customer value. Identifying and removing waste helps improve the overall development process.
* Build quality into the process instead of relying on testing at the end to find defects.
* Deliver software in small increments to get early feedback and adapt quickly to changing requirements.
* Continuously learn and improve by experimenting, gathering feedback, and refining the process.
* Optimize the whole value stream instead of improving individual steps in isolation, as local optimizations may not improve the overall system.
* Empower teams to make decisions, collaborate effectively, and take ownership of delivering value.
