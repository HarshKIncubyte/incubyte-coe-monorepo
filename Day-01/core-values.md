# Succeeding at Incubyte - Core Values

## Key Notes

* **Relentless Pursuit of Quality with Pragmatism:** Always aim for high-quality solutions while keeping business needs and timelines in mind. Avoid unnecessary complexity.
* **Extreme Ownership:** Take responsibility for your work, decisions, and outcomes. Be proactive and don't wait for others to solve problems.
* **Proactive Collaboration:** Share knowledge, communicate openly, and work together to achieve the best results.
* **Active Pursuit of Mastery:** Continuously improve both technical and soft skills through learning, practice, and feedback.
* **Give, Invite & Act on Feedback:** Give constructive feedback, actively seek feedback, and use it to improve yourself and your work.
* **Create Ecstatic Customers:** Focus on understanding customer needs and delivering solutions that exceed their expectations.
