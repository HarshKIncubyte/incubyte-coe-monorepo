# Kaizen

## Key Notes

* **Kaizen** means *"change for the better"* and is commonly understood as **continuous improvement**.
* It is based on making **small, incremental improvements** consistently instead of relying on large, one-time changes.
* The idea is that small improvements, when made continuously, lead to significant improvements in quality, efficiency, and performance over time.
* Kaizen encourages everyone in the team to identify problems, suggest improvements, and actively participate in improving the way work is done.
* Removing waste and unnecessary activities helps teams use their time, effort, and resources more effectively.
* Standardized processes make it easier to identify issues, solve problems faster, and continuously optimize the process.
* In software development, Kaizen promotes continuous reflection, regular process improvement, collaboration, and continuous learning throughout the development lifecycle.
